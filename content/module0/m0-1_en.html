Course Objectives

As a procurement specialist, it is important that you understand your responsibilities and accountabilities as a steward of public funds and ensure that each procurement project is treated in an open, transparent, and fair manner.

Upon completion of this course, you will be able to:

  • Define the roles and responsibilities of procurement specialists, key stakeholders and partners involved in the procurement process
  • Identify the various means of oversight and reporting requirements that strengthen government accountability in procurement projects
  • Distinguish among the different contracting authorities related to procuring goods and services
  • Identify the underlying principles, legislation and policies related to procurement
