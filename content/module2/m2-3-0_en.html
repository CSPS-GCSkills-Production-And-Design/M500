Reporting Requirements on Contracting Activities

Departments and agencies are responsible for making available various reports on contracting activities. Contracting officers must ensure the accuracy of the data contained in the contract as this information is used in a variety of reports and posted on websites as required to meet legal, policy, corporate, and parliamentary needs.

On the next couple of pages, you will be introduced to the following different types of reports:

  • Proactive Disclosure of Contracts
  • Contracts with Former Public Servants
  • Comprehensive Land Claims Agreements
  • Procurement Strategy for Aboriginal Business
  • Government Contracts and Real Property Leases in the Nunavut Settlement Area
  • Ownership of Intellectual Property
  • Auditor General Reports
  • Internal Audit Reports
