Summary

Now that you have completed this module, you are better positioned to understand how the scrutiny of government procurement and the reporting requirements impact your day‑to‑day procurement and financial management activities and how they contribute to all the organizational reports produced by your organization.

A few reminders:

  • Ensure financial delegations and accountabilities are clear and respected.
  • Consult key players that also have responsibilities and accountabilities related to procurement.
  • Create an environment of due diligence.
  • Properly document your files and keep a copy of key documents.

In the next module, you will learn about the delegation of authority related to procurement.
