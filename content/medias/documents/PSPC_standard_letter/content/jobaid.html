Delegation of Authority Letter from the Minister of Public Services and Procurement

I, (Name of Minister), Minister of Public Services and Procurement, pursuant to subsection 8(1) of the Department of Public Works and Government Services Act, do hereby delegate to the Minister of , powers related to the acquisition and provision of articles, supplies, machinery, equipment and other materiel for the use of departments for which he or she is the appropriate minister within the meaning of the Financial Administration Act, as follows:

  1. the power to enter into contracts if the cumulative amount payable under each contract and its amendments does not exceed $25,000;
  2. notwithstanding paragraph 1, the delegate may enter into a contract where the amount payable exceeds $25,000 if the contract is pursuant to a Supply Arrangement or takes the form of a call up on a Standing Offer established with Public Services and Procurement Canada;
  3. notwithstanding paragraph 1, the delegate may enter into a contract to acquire articles, supplies, machinery, equipment and other materiel where the amount payable exceeds $25,000, if the articles, supplies, machinery, equipment or other materiel are required as a result of a pressing emergency in which delay would be injurious to the public interest.

Dated at Gatineau this day of 20XX.


(Name of Minister)
Minister of Public Services and Procurement
