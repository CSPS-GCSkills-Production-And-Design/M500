Summary

Now that you have completed this module, you are better positioned to ensure the proper application of delegation of authority throughout the procurement process. You can also differentiate between contracting for goods and services and ensure people act within their contracting limits.

Knowing where your authorities come from, understanding the roles and responsibilities of key players, and the hierarchical breakdown of the rules that govern your actions are essential to procuring in the federal public service.

In the next module, through a series of short scenarios, you will integrate and apply what you have learned in the previous modules. This scenario-type approach will help you better understand the different aspects of the legal framework that guides government procurement.
