Module : 1. Roles and Responsibilities

Overview

There are a number of key players involved in the procurement field, and it is important to understand the various roles and responsibilities from both strategic and operational perspectives. Knowing who does what will make your job much easier.

At the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • define the roles and responsibilities of specialists involved in the procurement community
  • identify the stakeholders and their roles in the procurement process
  • recognize the role played by strategic partners in the procurement process

This module will take approximately 15 to 20 minutes to complete.
